Dropping All Pretences

Girish Borkar

·

3 min read
·
Feb 11, 2024

--

In the journey of spiritual awakening, one of the most transformative practices is the act of dropping all pretences. It is a profound shift from living in a state of illusion to embracing the truth of our being. Pretences, often built upon societal expectations, fears, and insecurities, veil our authentic selves, hindering our spiritual growth and connection with the divine.

Authenticity is the cornerstone of spiritual evolution. It requires courage and vulnerability to strip away the layers of pretence and reveal our true essence. When we let go of the need to conform to external standards and expectations, we liberate ourselves from the shackles of societal conditioning. We become free to express our thoughts, emotions, and beliefs authentically, without fear of judgment or rejection.

Dropping all pretences invites us to cultivate a deep sense of self-awareness. It prompts us to explore the depths of our being, confront our fears and insecurities, and embrace our imperfections with love and compassion. Through this process of self-discovery, we come to recognise that our true worth lies not in our external achievements or appearances but in the essence of our being.

At the core of dropping all pretences lies the practice of radical honesty. It requires us to be truthful not only with others but also with ourselves. Radical honesty demands that we acknowledge our limitations, mistakes, and shortcomings without shame or guilt. It is through embracing our vulnerabilities that we open ourselves up to healing and transformation.

In the realm of spirituality, dropping all pretences is synonymous with surrender. It is surrendering the ego’s need to control and manipulate our experiences and outcomes. Surrendering to the divine allows us to align with the flow of life, trusting that everything unfolds according to a higher plan. It is in this state of surrender that we find true peace, acceptance, and inner harmony.

The path of dropping all pretences is not always easy. It requires us to confront our deepest fears and insecurities, to let go of the masks we wear to hide our true selves. Yet, it is through this process of shedding the layers of illusion that we uncover the beauty and authenticity of our souls.

In dropping all pretences, we also let go of the need for external validation. We no longer seek approval or validation from others to feel worthy. Instead, we find validation from within, knowing that our worth is inherent and unconditional.

Dropping all pretences is an ongoing journey, a continuous process of peeling away the layers of conditioning and rediscovering our true essence. It is a journey of self-discovery, self-acceptance, and self-love. It is about embracing our uniqueness, celebrating our authenticity, and living our lives in alignment with our deepest truths.

As we embark on this journey of dropping all pretences, let us remember that authenticity is not about being perfect but about being real. It is about embracing our flaws and imperfections, knowing that they are what make us human. It is about living with integrity, honesty, and courage, trusting that in our authenticity, we find true liberation and fulfilment.
